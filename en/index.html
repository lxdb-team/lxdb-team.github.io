About LXDB

Wahat is LXDB ?

We are a team of technically enthusiastic young people from Germany who are committing to free Software and would like to contribute something to it.

What are we doing ?

We write programs, create Tutorials, make Videos about FOSS, and much more. All our offers can be viewed under Services.

Our software is free

The software we’re creating is ditributed under the OpenSource license GNU GPLv3.0.

Available for everyone.

Available under an OpenSource-license our software is usable by everyone.

Customizable by everyone.

Free.

Under the terms of an OpenSource license, our software will stay free forever.

Efficient developable
